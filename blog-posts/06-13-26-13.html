I'm rediscovering my joy of software.

June 13, 2026 · 1 min read

This probably doesn't need to be an item on this but as of recent I've kind of been rediscovering my initial joy in learning about software, which is really exciting. It's really easy to get stuck in a thought process of you need to know everything and then just stop pursuing anything because of how much there is to learn, probably just my perfectionist mindset. I've had a lot of fun learning about networking and some more niche cloud computing concepts rather than trying to deploy another basic SaaS. I'll see where this takes me though, but to all, have fun learning. I'll be posting my findings here.
